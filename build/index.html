I'm a Software Engineer and Engineering Manager from Erie, PA. I specialize in architecting and developing web applications of a variety of sizes, and leading teams to do the same. I've primarily worked in JavaScript, Ruby, and PHP over the past several years.

I'm an Engineering Lead at Auth0, where I work with a globally distributed team to help make identity easy. I also run a small screencast site, Bits and Trees, and host a podcast under the same name. Locally I organize the annual Erie Day of Code conference with a group of awesome developers.

I like to speak at conferences, work on open source, and write about JavaScript, testing, and other related topics. Sometimes I turn them into bigger projects. If any of that sounds interesting, please sign up for my newsletter to keep tabs on my activies.
